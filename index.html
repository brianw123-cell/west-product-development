About

Who we are

West Product Development brings together deep mechanical engineering expertise and modern software architecture — auditing legacy workflows and implementing AI-driven tools to accelerate the product development lifecycle. We focus on renewable energy, solar, and climate-sector businesses, where outdated spreadsheets and paper-based processes slow down teams doing work that matters.

Brian West
Principal Engineer & AI Automation Consultant

With over 25 years of experience across Consumer Products, Machinery, and Renewables, Brian combines expert-level mechanical design with a sharp focus on modernizing the engineering workflow. He researches and applies AI-driven tools to automate the Product Lifecycle — helping transform how hardware products go from concept to production. Brian’s current work centers on replacing manual business workflows with automated digital systems built on Google Workspace, Apps Script, and custom web applications.

Jennifer Luna
Systems Architect & Software Engineering Lead

Jennifer brings over 15 years of expertise in software architecture and development, with hands-on experience across full-stack web development, CI/CD, DevOps, and scalable infrastructure design.
